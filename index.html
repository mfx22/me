Interests

Apart from my interests in building and creating, I enjoy spending my time with my friends and family, trying out new hobbies. From the Appalachian Mountains in Southwest Virginia, I have always enjoyed being active, spending my time outdoors hiking and exploring. Rock climbing has been a continued passion of mine and I have been actively climbing for over 4 years now, both bouldering and sport climbing.

When not busy climbing or tinkering, I can be found playing video games, board games, or following my newfound interests in zcooking. Currently, I am trying to perfect cuisine combining my family’s traditional Asian recipes with more American ingredients and cooking techniques. I also enjoy shopping at local thrift stores, searching for good deals and steals. Recently, I have been spending a large amount of my free time pursuing digital art and design both for my own interests and campus organizations.
